# Research Documents

Standalone analysis or investigation. Not spec (CLAUDE.md), not vision (README). Time-boxed thinking captured for reference.

---

# STRUCTURE

1. **Question** — What this investigates. One sentence.
2. **Findings** — What was discovered. Evidence and reasoning.
3. **Conclusions** — Actionable takeaways. What to do next.

Order fixed. Sections scale — a quick investigation may be three paragraphs; a deep dive may have subsections under Findings.

---

# LIFECYCLE

Findings may graduate:
- Into CLAUDE.md as decisions (settled choices with rationale)
- Into README as context (business motivation)
- Into code as implementation

Research docs that have fully graduated can be deleted. The conclusions live in their destination.

---

# VALIDATION

- Clear question stated up front
- Conclusions are actionable
- Not duplicating content that belongs in CLAUDE.md or README
